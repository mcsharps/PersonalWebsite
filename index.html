About Me!

I am personally and professionally dedicated to the notion of life long learning. I believe through learning and practice I can share with the world creations that others can use. I am naturally curious, this is evident in the hobbies I pursue which can be read more about on my hobbies page. As someone who is constantly working to improve my programming skills and develop my own style it brings me great joy to be a part of communities like github where I can post my work, read about, and get involved with other projects where I can become part of a larger programming community. As open software and open hardware (a la arduino, raspberry pi, and other microcontrollers) become more prevalent I hope we can utilize these technologies to bring us closer together as humans and advance into the future with ease.
